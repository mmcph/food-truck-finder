Personas

Customer: Art Vandalay

Age: 19

Technology: IPhone 7 Plus / iOS 11.3.1

Bio: Art is a single construction worker who works in different areas of the city depending on where his abilities are needed for jobs.

Goals: Art is looking to find a nearby food truck to grab a bite during his lunch hour.

Frustrations: Art has had issues in the past with returning late to work due to spending a majority of his lunch seeking a place to eat.

User Stories

Customer: As a hungry person, I want a simple way to find the nearest good food trucks.

Customer: As a recently satiated person, I want a way to add the bomb-ass food truck I just ate at to a list of favorites for later reference.

Interaction Flows

  • Persona: Art Vandalay
  • Description: Searching for food trucks
  • Preconditions: Art is hungry, so he uses his Food Truck Finder app to search for nearby food trucks.
  • Postconditions: Art finds an operational food truck near his location.
  1. Art signs in to the app.
  2. Server sends Art the landing page
  3. (assuming Art has allowed the app to use his location data) Google Maps populates embedded map with all food trucks currently operational; viewport centered on Art’s location
  4. Art selects a food truck that interests him.
  5. Server loads the profile for that food truck, including the address.
  6. Art heads in that direction.

  • Persona: Art Vandalay
  • Description: Add food truck to favorites
  • Preconditions: Art is registered on the site.
  • Postconditions: Art has added a favorite to the list.
  1. Art signs in to the site.
  2. Server displays nearby food trucks.
  3. Art clicks on the search button and enters "Bob's Tacos"
  4. Server returns back search results.
  5. Art clicks on "Bob's Tacos" profile
  6. Server sends back Bob's Tacos profile.
  7. Art clicks on the button “Rate Bob’s Tacos”
  8. Server returns back stars available to rate.
  9. Art clicks on the 5th star and hits “save”
  10. 1 Server saves the 5stars to Bob’s profile.
