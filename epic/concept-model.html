Conceptual Model for our Food Truck Finder

  • Profile
    • profileId (uuid)(primary key)
    • profileEmail (varchar 128)
    • profileHash (char 97)
    • profileIsOwner (tinyint)
    • profileFirstName (varchar 64)
    • profileLastName (varchar 64)
    • profileUserName (varchar 64)
  • Category (Enumeration)
    • categoryId (int unsigned auto_increment not null)(primary key)
    • categoryName(varchar 32)
  • Truck
    • truckId (uuid)(primary key)
    • truckProfileId (uuid)(foreign key)
    • truckBio(varchar 1024)
    • truckIsOpen(tinyint)
    • truckLatitude(decimal(10, 8)
    • truckLongitude(decimal(11, 8))
    • truckName(varchar 64)
    • truckPhone(varchar 24)
    • truckUrl(varchar 128)
  • Vote
    • voteProfileId (uuid)(foreign key)
    • voteTruckId (uuid)(foreign key)
    • voteValue(tinyint(1 || -1))
  • TruckCategory
    • truckCategoryCategoryId (tinyint)(foreign key)
    • truckCategoryTruckId (uuid)(foreign key)
  • Favorite
    • favoriteTruckId (uuid)(foreign key)
    • favoriteProfileId (uuid)(foreign key)
